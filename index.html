Why

We are here to do our part for our planet Earth (with a little help from you, of course): to substitute single-use plastic water bottles in the F&B industry with eco-friendly and sustainable glass bottles.

Did you know that glass bottles are considered the most environmentally friendly when it comes to storing everyday beverages such as water? ‘Surayee’ means an earthen pot; and through us, you get the best of both worlds – our products contain all the goodness of alkaline water packaged in a way that is good for the Earth.

What’s better? We are affordable because we truly believe that sustaining our climate and natural resources shouldn’t come at a large premium.
